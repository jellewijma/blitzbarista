Café Tycoon

A Strategic Business Simulation Game

Project Overview

Welcome to Café Tycoon, a strategic business simulation game where players manage their own café and compete against other businesses on the Zuidplein. Players must navigate rising rental costs and aim to outlast their competitors.

Key Features

  • Manage and grow your own café.
  • Navigate monthly increasing rental costs.
  • Compete against other businesses on the Zuidplein.
  • Outlast competitors or face game over when unable to pay rent.
